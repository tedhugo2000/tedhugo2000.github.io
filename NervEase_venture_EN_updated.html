The Patient Dilemma
Current solutions force patients to compromise.
Patients don’t abandon care because it doesn’t work — they abandon it because it doesn’t work when they need it.

Medication Risks

NSAIDs and Opioids carry severe risks of addiction, stomach ulcers, and kidney damage. Patients are desperate to "get off the pills."

TENS Limitations

Traditional TENS devices (like Omron) stop working the moment you turn them off. They mask pain but don't treat the tissue.

PBM Slowness

Light therapy (PBM) heals, but takes weeks to show results. Patients lose patience and abandon the treatment due to lack of immediate feedback.

The Gap: Patients need Instant Relief AND Long-term Healing.
